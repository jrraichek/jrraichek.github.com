Mom's Banana Bread

Ingredients

  1. 2/3C Sugar
  2. 1/3C Oil or Butter
  3. 2 Eggs
  4. 3T Sour Milk
  5. 1C Mashed Bananas
  6. 2C Flour
  7. 1/2t Baking Soda
  8. 1t Baking Powder
  9. 1/2t Salt

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Begin by mixing together the sugar, oil, eggs, sour milk, and mashed bananas into a large bowl. In a separate bowl mix together flour, backing soda, backing powder, and salt. Gradual add the dry ingredients into the wet until mixed thoroughly.
